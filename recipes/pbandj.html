Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Easy as can be and perhaps the most classic American lunch there is. Not much to say here, I wish you all the luck on your peanut butter and jelly journey.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of the bread of your choice
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly/Jam/Preserves of choice

Steps:

  1. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
  2. Spread jelly/jam/preserves on the other slice of bread
  3. Put the slices of bread together with the peanut butter and jelly sides touching
  4. Enjoy!
